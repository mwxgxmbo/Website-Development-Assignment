A Firm Built on Trust, Sharpened by Experience

Founded by Senior Counsel Emmanuel Mwagambo and Partner Donald Okonjo, our firm has grown from a boutique Nairobi practice into one of East Africa's respected legal houses. We combine deep local knowledge with international standards of legal excellence.
