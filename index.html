Who We Are

A Firm Built on Integrity, Driven by Results

Founded in 2007 as Mwagambo & Okonjo Advocates, the firm rebranded to Mwagambo & Okonjo LLP in 2022 following a strategic restructuring that expanded our practice areas and modernised our corporate structure.

Based in Nairobi's Kilimani area with a branch in Malindi, we serve domestic and international companies, banks, development finance agencies, NGOs, and county governments. We also act as Kenyan counsel for partner firms in South Africa, Uganda, and Tanzania.

Our Full Story

Corporate & Commercial Practice

Due diligence, mergers and acquisitions, banking and financial transactions, public listings, rights issues, asset sales, and syndicate loan financing. We have handled registration of major banks, ICT companies, and Africa's first futures exchange.

Learn more

Litigation & Arbitration

Representation before Kenyan Courts, Tribunals, and in Arbitration. We have successfully defended institutions in suits worth Ksh. 90 Million and are handling a Ksh. 272 Million compulsory acquisition matter. We actively explore ADR for our clients.

Learn more

Commercial Property & Real Estate

Financing for real estate development, commercial leases, large-scale acquisitions, planning permissions, and environmental compliance. We have advised on developments from Karen to Mombasa Road with transactions worth hundreds of millions of shillings.

Learn more

Employment & Labour Law

Drafting employment contracts and workplace policies, advising county governments and corporates, handling unfair dismissal and labour disputes before the Employment and Labour Relations Court.

Learn more

Constitutional & Judicial Review

Civil, constitutional, and judicial review litigation. Governance, policy, and legislative advisory for public and private sector clients. Electoral law and mediation as a preferred mechanism for dispute resolution.
