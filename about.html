Our History

From Boutique Practice to Trusted Institution

Mwagambo & Okonjo LLP is a Kenyan commercial law firm providing a comprehensive range of quality legal services and solutions for our local and international clients. The firm was originally established in 2007 under the name Mwagambo & Okonjo Advocates.

In 2022, as part of a strategic restructuring and growth initiative, the firm was rebranded and renamed Mwagambo & Okonjo LLP, reflecting its expanded practice areas and modernised corporate structure. This transition was formalised with our registration as a Limited Liability Partnership (LLP-6R1KKD6) under the Limited Liability Partnerships Act, 2011.

We deliver legal solutions that are practical, innovative, specialised, and cost-effective. The firm has built a reputation for providing specialised business advisory services, top-notch litigation, and a broad range of corporate and commercial legal services.

Milestones

Our Journey

2007

Firm established in Nairobi as Mwagambo & Okonjo Advocates by Emmanuel Mwagambo and Donald O. Okonjo.

2016

Expanded commercial practice, facilitating major joint ventures and grain handling terminal establishment in Mombasa.

2022

Strategic restructuring — firm rebranded and registered as Mwagambo & Okonjo LLP (Reg. No. LLP-6R1KKD6).

Present

Operating from Nairobi and Malindi, serving clients across Kenya and as regional counsel in East and Southern Africa.

Our People

Our Team & Expertise

Our partners and associates have varied educational backgrounds with unique exposure to the Kenyan and other legal systems, helping the firm better understand our clients' businesses and provide solutions in diverse areas such as taxation, civil aviation, banking, finance, insurance, energy, telecommunications, and transportation.

To respond effectively to the ever-changing legal needs of the business environment in the region, the firm places great emphasis on continuous learning to supplement the advanced and specialised degrees that our Partners and Associates hold.

Experienced partners, who remain closely involved in all aspects of client work, lead all transaction teams. We also work with diverse consultants in tax and international finance when handling large and complex matters.
