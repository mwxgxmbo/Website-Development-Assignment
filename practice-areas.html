We offer a sophisticated and comprehensive range of services to clients who include domestic and international companies, banks, development finance agencies, and non-governmental organisations across all sectors of the economy. The firm also works as Kenyan counsel for partner law firms in South Africa, Uganda, and Tanzania and their clients.

Corporate & Commercial Practice

Our commercial practice handles a range of county government, corporate, banking, and financial transactions. We provide commercial advisory services including due diligence, negotiating restructurings, advising on public listings, rights issues, asset sales, and financing through single lender or syndicate loans.

  • Registration of a Representative Office for a leading regional bank
  • Oversight and advisory on a major engineering company merger & acquisition
  • Advising on the registration of a local bank, including a preliminary banking license
  • Facilitating the set-up of Kenya's largest and leading ICT company
  • Currently involved in the set-up of Africa's first futures exchange and derivatives market
  • Facilitating incorporation of a mobile money payment company in Kenya
  • Trademark and patent registration for leading regional brands
  • Facilitated acquisition of 75% of issued share capital by a foreign investor
  • Facilitated joint venture establishment and financing for top-tier local companies

Litigation & Arbitration

With a fast-growing reputation for top-notch litigation, our litigation department handles matters before the Kenyan Courts, various Tribunals, and represents clients in Arbitration. We give realistic advice from the initial dispute situation through negotiation, trial, and beyond — always considering alternative dispute resolution given the delays in Kenya's court system.

  • Successful defense of a leading commercial bank in a suit for Ksh. 20 Million
  • Successful defense of a parastatal in a Ksh. 90 Million suit, securing victory for the client
  • Currently handling a multi-million Government Compulsory Acquisition Suit
  • Handling a confidential Land Dispute matter valued at Ksh. 272 Million
  • We serve on the Panel of Advocates of various financial institutions
  • Advisory on environmental audit and impact assessment compliance under Kenyan environmental law

Commercial Property & Real Estate Advisory

The Real Estate and Construction Sector continues to grow exponentially in Kenya. M&O remains the firm of choice for property developers, construction companies, and investors handling complex structures involving financing, regulatory matters, large-scale sale and purchase, sale and lease-back arrangements, tax, and related issues.

Our Services Cover:
  • Financing for real estate development
  • Drafting of commercial leases and related documents
  • Re-structuring of medium and large-scale developments
  • Large-scale acquisitions and dispositions
  • Planning and development permissions, including environmental compliance
Recent Transactions:
  • Advised a developer on 15 high-end units in Karen at Ksh. 60 million each
  • Advised a China-based developer on a 300-unit apartment development along Mombasa Road
  • Advised on a housing & shopping complex with six residential estates, a mall, and office block

Employment & Labour Law

We advise corporates, county governments, and individuals on employment contracts, workplace policies, and labour disputes. Our team represents clients before the Employment and Labour Relations Court and provides strategic advisory on unfair dismissal claims and regulatory compliance.

  • Drafting and review of employment contracts and workplace policies
  • Representation in unfair dismissal and labour dispute matters
  • Advisory for county governments and corporate HR departments
  • Mediation and negotiation in workplace disputes

Constitutional & Judicial Review

Our partners bring deep experience in constitutional law, judicial review, and electoral law, alongside governance, policy, and legislative advisory for public and private sector clients. We assist with mediation, negotiation, and alternative dispute resolution as part of a broader strategic approach to complex legal matters.

  • Civil, constitutional, and judicial review litigation
  • Governance, policy, and legislative advisory
  • Electoral law advisory
  • Mediation, negotiation, and alternative dispute resolution
  • Strategic advisory for public and private sector clients
